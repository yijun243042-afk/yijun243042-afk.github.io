Jay Chou (周杰伦)

Jay Chou is a Taiwanese singer, songwriter, actor, and director who has been one of Asia’s most influential artists since the early 2000s. Known as the "King of Mandopop", he blends pop, R&B, hip-hop, and traditional Chinese music in a unique and innovative way. Chou debuted in 2000 with his album Jay, and quickly gained fame for his poetic lyrics and experimental sound. His creativity extends beyond music to film, where he has starred in and directed several successful movies. Over the years, Jay Chou has not only redefined Chinese pop music but also inspired a new generation of musicians and artists worldwide.
